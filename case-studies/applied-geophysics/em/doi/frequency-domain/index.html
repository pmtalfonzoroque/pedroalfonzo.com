Frequency‑Domain Electromagnetics

Frequency‑domain EM systems use sinusoidal sources to probe the subsurface. The relationship between frequency, attenuation, and penetration depth provides a complementary perspective to the time‑domain formulation.

Abstract

This module introduces the concept of depth of investigation (DOI) in the frequency domain. By examining amplitude and phase responses across frequencies, we explore how conductivity structure influences penetration depth and resolution.
